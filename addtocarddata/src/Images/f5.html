Khari Sing is a roasted salty peanut. It contains high amount of nutritional value. A combination of khari sing-daliya (roasted chana) is a popular form of healthy snack liked by people of all ages. Peanuts are frequently used in sweet dishes, snacks, milk shakes and many more food preparations for their taste and for their nutritional value.

Peanuts are always a part of Indian snacks . Peanuts are coated with some spices to make a delicious and yummy snack which is high in protein and comfortable to eat.
